Contact the League of Arab States

For institutional inquiries, public information requests, and general correspondence, the League welcomes communication from partners, media, and the public.

Reaching the League

The League of Arab States is committed to maintaining clear and professional communication with institutions, media organizations, and members of the public. Whether you are seeking information about our work, requesting a meeting, or exploring collaboration opportunities, the appropriate channels are available to assist you.

We encourage all enquiries to be sent through official contact points so that requests can be handled efficiently and directed to the relevant department.

  • General inquiries may be directed to the League’s official information and correspondence channels.
  • Media requests should be submitted through the designated communications contact.
  • Institutional and partnership enquiries are directed to the relevant departmental offices.

Useful information

For the most efficient response, please include the nature of your enquiry and your preferred contact details.

  • Institutional requests
  • Media and public communications
  • General correspondence

Official communication

All correspondence is handled through formal institutional channels to ensure clarity, accuracy, and appropriate follow-up.
