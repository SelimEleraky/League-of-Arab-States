Careers and Internships at the League of Arab States

The League offers meaningful opportunities for professionals and emerging talent who wish to contribute to regional cooperation, institutional development, and public service across the Arab world.

Building careers around regional impact

At the League of Arab States, careers are shaped by purpose, collaboration, and a commitment to advancing shared regional priorities. Our work spans diplomacy, policy analysis, administration, communications, development, and technical cooperation, offering professionals the chance to contribute to initiatives that influence the future of the Arab world.

We value individuals who bring strong analytical ability, professionalism, and a genuine interest in public service. Whether working in a specialized technical role or supporting institutional operations, employees and interns help strengthen the League’s role as a platform for dialogue, coordination, and action.

  • Opportunities are available across policy, administration, research, communications, and program support.
  • We encourage applications from candidates with relevant academic credentials, professional experience, and a commitment to regional cooperation.
  • Internship programs provide practical exposure to the work of international institutions and the day-to-day operations of a major regional organization.

What we look for

Applicants are encouraged to demonstrate initiative, teamwork, and a strong understanding of the League’s mission and regional role.

  • Relevant academic background
  • Professional or research experience
  • Strong communication skills
  • Commitment to public service

Join a mission that matters

Whether you are an experienced professional or a young graduate seeking your first step into international public service, the League offers a platform to contribute meaningfully to regional cooperation and long-term development.

When the internship application window opens, applicants are invited to submit their CV to careers@lasapplication.com with the subject line: "[Desired Position] – [Full Name]".
